Professional Development

School Based Teacher Trainers we will provide face to face training development series covering administrative overview, system use, navigation and data analysis, progress report analysis, graduation tracking, teacher progress and accountability. Onsite (face-to-face) trainings allow new users an opportunity to explore and engage with the system under the direct guidance of a certified trainer. These one-day trainings include: research overview, step-by-step instruction on data collection and analysis, and technical guidance and support.

Quality Review Preparation

Quality Review Evaluator will help schools learn to evaluate data in their system, demonstrate how the data can be used to drive instruction and strengthening educators’ skills in assessing student, teacher and administration needs. The team will work with schools to provide a well-rounded perspective of the way in which schools can use information about outcomes to guide teaching, set goals for improvement, and make adjustments.

Quality Review Evaluator will assist schools to break down complex student data to inform and support instruction, professional development, curriculum planning, accountability, and policy. Making it easy to view, assess and cross-reference any data you choose to reveal the statistics, trends, challenges and successes your has accomplished for presentation for your QR evaluation . Team members will provide helpful suggestions along with a full walk through of the Quality Review Process.
